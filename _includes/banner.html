Eric Gibbons, PhD

Assistant Professor

Electrical and Computer Engineering

Weber State University

I am an imaging scientist specializing in magnetic resonance imaging. I am currently an assistant professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Weber State University. I recently finished my PhD in bioengineering/electrical engineering in the Magnetic Resonance Systems Research Laboratory at Stanford University under John Pauly. Prior to that, I earned my B.S. in biomedical engineering with a mathematics minor at the University of Utah as well as an M.S. in bioengineering at Stanford.
